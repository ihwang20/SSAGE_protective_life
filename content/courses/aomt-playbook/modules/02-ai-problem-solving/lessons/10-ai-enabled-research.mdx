Beyond analyzing internal data, AI dramatically accelerates external research — benchmarking against industry standards, understanding best practices, and identifying analogous situations from other organizations or industries.

<Callout type="tip" title="Benchmark Research at Speed">
A practitioner using AI can gather and synthesize industry benchmark data in hours that would previously take days of manual research. This dramatically changes when in the engagement benchmarking becomes feasible — making it a first-pass activity rather than a late-stage confirmation.
</Callout>

<Callout type="warning" title="Verify AI-Generated Benchmarks">
AI may generate plausible-sounding benchmark figures that are not accurate. Always verify key benchmark numbers against a named, verifiable source before using them in client-facing materials. Treat AI-generated benchmarks as starting hypotheses, not confirmed facts.
</Callout>
