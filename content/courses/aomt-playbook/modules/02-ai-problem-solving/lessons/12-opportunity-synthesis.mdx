Synthesis converts validated findings into clearly articulated opportunities. It is the bridge between diagnosis (what is wrong and why) and prioritization (what should we fix first and how).

<Callout type="concept" title="From Finding to Opportunity">
A finding answers: "What is happening?" An opportunity answers: "What should we do about it, and what value will that create?" Synthesis requires translating data patterns into actionable, quantified improvement opportunities with preliminary solution options.
</Callout>

## Synthesis Steps

<ProcessFlow steps={[
  { title: "Cluster Validated Findings", description: "Group related findings into thematic opportunities. Multiple findings may point to the same underlying opportunity." },
  { title: "Quantify the Impact", description: "For each opportunity cluster, calculate the financial and operational value of closing the gap. Use conservative, defensible assumptions." },
  { title: "Generate Solution Options", description: "Develop 2–4 preliminary solution options for each opportunity, ranging from quick wins to strategic investments." },
  { title: "Stakeholder Validation", description: "Pressure-test synthesis findings with stakeholders who have domain expertise. Their reactions often surface additional nuance or validate the magnitude of the opportunity." }
]} />
