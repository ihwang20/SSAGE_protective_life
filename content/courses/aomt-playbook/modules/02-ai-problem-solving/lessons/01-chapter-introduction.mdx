<Callout type="note" title="Metro Cable Case Study">
**Metro Cable** is a fictional cable and broadband services company used as the running case example throughout this module. **The Core Problem:** Metro Cable's first-visit resolution rate has declined from **78% to 62%** over the past 12 months. Repeat dispatches are increasing, field technicians are losing productive time, and customer dissatisfaction is rising. With a PSC Rate Case hearing in 12 months, the executive team needs demonstrated improvement quickly.
</Callout>

## What the Practitioner Team Does

- **Lesson 2.3 — Problem Statement:** Define the problem scope, boundaries, decision makers, and success measures
- **Lesson 2.4 — Issues Tree:** Decompose the resolution rate decline into 5 branches — Dispatch & Routing, Technician Capability, Parts Availability, Job Complexity, and Customer & Scheduling Factors — each with testable hypotheses
- **Lesson 2.5 — Data Request:** Identify and formally request 14 months of dispatch records, technician certification data, geographic zone maps, and customer satisfaction surveys
- **Lessons 2.7–2.8 — Analysis:** Use AI to run correlation analyses between technician travel distance, certification level, and first-visit resolution rates across service zones

<Callout type="concept" title="Two-Phase Approach">
AI-Empowered Problem Solving operates in two distinct phases. **Phase 1** focuses on iterative diagnosis — ingesting data, surfacing patterns, and synthesizing findings. **Phase 2** focuses on prioritizing the opportunities uncovered during diagnosis into an actionable roadmap.
</Callout>

## Phase 2: Prioritization

Once opportunities are identified and documented, Phase 2 applies structured scoring methods to rank them by value, complexity, and strategic alignment. This transforms a list of possibilities into a sequenced roadmap that stakeholders can review and act on.

<Callout type="tip" title="Why This Matters">
Traditional problem-solving can take weeks of interviews and workshops before a single insight emerges. With AI-empowered diagnosis, practitioners can surface data-driven insights within hours, then validate those insights with stakeholders — dramatically accelerating time to action.
</Callout>
