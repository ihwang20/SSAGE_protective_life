A data analysis plan translates hypotheses from the issues tree into specific analytical tasks. It specifies what will be analyzed, what method will be used, what data is required, and what finding would confirm or deny each hypothesis.

| Hypothesis | Analysis Method | Data Required | Confirming Finding |
|---|---|---|---|
| Geographic mismatch drives FCR decline | Correlation analysis: travel distance vs. FCR by zone | Dispatch records with GPS coordinates and FCR outcomes | Correlation coefficient > 0.6 between distance and FCR |
| Certification gaps cause repeat dispatches | Cross-tabulation: job type vs. technician certification vs. FCR | Technician certification records, job type classification | FCR rate < 60% for certification-mismatch jobs |
| Parts unavailability drives repeat visits | Frequency analysis: "parts not available" reason codes | Dispatch close codes and reason fields | "Parts unavailable" reason code > 20% of repeat visits |
