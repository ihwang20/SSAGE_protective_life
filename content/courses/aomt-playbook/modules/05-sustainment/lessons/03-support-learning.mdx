The Support pillar ensures that people have the knowledge, skills, and resources to use the AI solution effectively — not just at launch, but continuously as the solution evolves and staff turns over.

<Callout type="concept" title="Learning is Ongoing, Not One-Time">
A single training session at launch is insufficient. Knowledge decays, staff turns over, and the solution evolves. Effective support systems include initial training, ongoing coaching, job aids embedded in the workflow, communities of practice, and refresher training triggered by performance data.
</Callout>

## Support System Components

<ProcessFlow steps={[
  { title: "Initial Training", description: "Role-specific training that covers the what, why, and how. Not just \"how to use the tool\" but \"why this approach produces better outcomes.\"" },
  { title: "Job Aids and Quick References", description: "Embedded reference materials that users can access in the moment of need — decision guides, exception handling procedures, escalation paths." },
  { title: "Communities of Practice", description: "Regular forums where practitioners share experiences, surface emerging issues, and collectively develop expertise. These build the organizational knowledge base that survives individual turnover." },
  { title: "Performance-Triggered Coaching", description: "When monitoring systems detect declining performance (e.g., a technician's FCR drops below threshold), a targeted coaching intervention is triggered rather than waiting for the next scheduled training cycle." }
]} />
