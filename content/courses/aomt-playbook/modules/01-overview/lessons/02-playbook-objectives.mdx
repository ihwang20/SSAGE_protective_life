## About the Metro Cable Case Study

The case study used in this playbook is a fictional but representative example based on a cable and internet provider. Metro Cable serves customers in the Kansas City metropolitan area, regulated by a Public Service Commission (PSC) which ensures quality service at reasonable prices.

Metro Cable is facing rising operational costs and declining customer satisfaction scores. They fear poor satisfaction will result in their requested rate increase being denied. A problem-solving team is assigned to determine root causes of low customer satisfaction.

The team discovers that a key driver of customer satisfaction is the consistent and predictable fulfillment of service appointment requests, and that late/missed appointments are related to how appointments are assigned to technicians and travel time between appointments.
