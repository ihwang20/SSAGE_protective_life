# Metro Cable Capstone Exercise

Step back from any single phase and evaluate the Metro Cable engagement as a complete methodology cycle.

<Callout type="concept" title="The Complete Engagement Arc">
The Metro Cable engagement moved from a vague operational complaint to a validated root cause, a prioritized roadmap, a designed AI solution, a sustainment framework, and a change management plan. Every phase depended on the one before it. This exercise asks you to trace those dependencies and apply the same logic to your own work.
</Callout>
